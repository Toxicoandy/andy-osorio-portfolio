Elección Tawaqo 2023

Strategic communication project focused on strengthening credibility, visibility and public confidence during the Tawaqo election process.

Large-scale cultural communication and photography project supporting one of La Paz's most important folkloric celebrations.

Responsible for official photographic coverage from the opening ceremony, including activities involving municipal authorities and participating groups.

The event attracted tens of thousands of spectators and received coverage from television, digital media and press organizations.

The resulting content helped increase public interest in folkloric groups, encouraged cultural participation and strengthened the visibility of the festival.
